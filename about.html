We are IT and accounting consultants firm

Established in May 2018 under the Business Names Registration Act (Cap. 213). It is registered as a firm of professional accountants by the National Board of Accountants and Auditors in Tanzania (NBAA) on 13th July 2018.

We're committed to providing a first-class service tailored to the individual needs of each client where all our costs are fixed – so you know in advance exactly how much you are paying. Looking after all your financial requirements, allowing you the real-time financial control that your business needs without the expense.

Our Mission

To serve and protect the interest of our customers by advising them to building value, improving performance, and improvising associated risks.

Our Vision

To be the best consulting firm in Tanzania and providing high-quality services to our clients.

Core Value

Quality and Accuracy

We provide our clients with an accounting, tax, and financial perspective that is thorough, accurate, and correct the first time and every time.

Responsiveness

No matter how big or small your question, issue, or situation is, we are committed to being responsive to our clients' needs.

Client Best Interest

We believe in putting our clients’ best interests first: we will always recommend and prescribe the services which you need, and nothing more.

Transparency

We are fully committed to being open, honest, and candid about our clients’ businesses and financial matters.
