      • About Us

      Abood Group of Companies is a family owned business established in 1986 in Morogoro, Tanzania.

      The group started as a regional trading house dealing in general goods trading in soft commodities and then expanded into its flag ship company in Passenger Transportation. As the group continued to grow year on year it diversified into other industries including Passenger transportation, Cargo transportation, Construction, Petroleum, Tourism, Media broadcast, General good trading and Real Estate. Abood Group transformation into a rapidly growing business group is widely considered to be an inspirational success story from its humble beginnings. Today, after 34 years it is a trusted brand the serves thousands of customers daily making it one of the largest business group in terms of customer base in Tanzania.

      Corporate Social Responsibility

      Our group aspires to significantly improve the lives of Tanzanians through our CSR goals and initiatives. We have supported various initiatives in the Morogoro region, predominantly focusing on healthcare, clean water and quality education. We have through our donation schemes to various local community groups donated Medical supplies and equipment and Borehole Drilling at various areas in Morogoro for access to clean water and supported educational schemes for children. Our various efforts through Abood Group have impacted the lives of over 60,000 people over the last 15 years in Morogoro Region.
