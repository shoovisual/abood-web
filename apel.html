    Apel Petroleum Limited

    Apel Petroleum Limited is an oil marketing company registered in Tanzania in 2006. Its operations are based at Kurasini, Dares salaam. The company handles importation, warehousing, distribution and marketing of petroleum products across Tanzania.

    The company has expanded its footprint across Tanzania establishing retail stations across the country and intends to become a key player in the petroleum sector in Tanzania. The company boast a large team of experienced professionals in the petroleum industry who ensure timely delivery of petroleum products to our clients.
