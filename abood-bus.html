Overview

Abood Bus Service Limited is the flagship company of Abood Group and is the largest intercirty bus operator in Tanzania. Founded in 1986, the company has built a solid reputation for quality and reliability, operating a fleet of more than 100 buses that serve the entire country, from the Northern to the Southern, Western and Eastern regions. At Abood Bus Service, we are committed to providing our passengers with the best possible experience.
